It's Starbucks

Starbucks, American company that is the largest coffeehouse chain and one of the most recognizable brands in the world. Its headquarters are in Seattle, Washington. Starbucks serves hot and cold drinks, whole-bean coffee, micro-ground instant coffee, espresso, caffe latte, full and loose-leaf teas, juices, Frappuccino beverages, pastries, and snacks.
